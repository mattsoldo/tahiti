Bora Bora - Mount Otemanu Climb

‚õ∞Ô∏è Mount Otemanu Climb (MAJOR ACTIVITY)

‚ö†Ô∏è Book guide in advance through Marie or your hotel

  • Bora Bora's tallest peak (727m / 2,385 feet)
  • Hike to the "shoulders" and sacred cave (can't reach actual summit)
  • Duration: 5-6 hours round trip
  • Difficulty: Strenuous - rope-assisted sections
  • Requirements: Good fitness level, proper hiking shoes
  • Views: Spectacular panoramas of lagoon, Taha'a, Raiatea, and Tupai atoll
